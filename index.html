The Course

Tech companies recruit an apprentice (like me) for a 2 year programme with the assistance of FIT. The first 6 months require apprentices to attend full time, off the job training. This takes place in the Regional Skills and Training Centre in Dundalk, Co. Louth where we are tutored by Dean Harrison of Oiliuna . The next 7 - 18 months includes working at my company, Intact, for 3 days a week and then 2 days in the RSTC. Months 19 - 24 are broken up into 4 day periods at our companies and 1 day in the RSTC. After 2 years, we then work full time as qualified (hopefully) software developers.

My Company

I was very fortunate that Intact were willing to take a chance with me and employ me as their apprentice. They requested someone who was a complete beginner but had an interest in the world of technology and a proven track record of self taught learning and education. The company HQ is based in my hometown of Dundalk, Co. Louth and it has offices all across Ireland and the UK. They have over 26 years experience in the field of enterprise resource planning and they supply a wide array of small and medium businesses with their software. In laymans terms, Intact develops software that organisations and companies use to manage day-to-day business activities, such as accounting, procurement, project management, risk management and compliance, and supply chain operations.
